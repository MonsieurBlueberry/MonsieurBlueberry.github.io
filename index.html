My First Post

28 MARCH 2021

Hi everyone! My name is Anya and this is my first ever post on my blog!

It is Sunday. Today, I learned about font families and other font styling tools in CSS. In addition, I asked a question for public answer for the first time ever on Khan Academy! The question follows:

Is there a way I can make a font less weighted? So, the opposite of "bold"?

Hey, I just came back after taking a nice, long break from my computer. Turns out I already have a response to my question! The response follows:

In order to do that, you would set the font-weight to something more than 400 (regular weight) and less than 900 (bold weight). But the opposite of bold is 100 (light weight).

Hope this helps!

That really helped me! I want to give a special thanks to "Malicia | Peace" for responding to my question with a detailed and helpful answer. I am especially thankful for her quick response! See you soon!
